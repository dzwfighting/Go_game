Go (game)

About Haifeng

In 1986, based on his love for Go, Mr. Lin Wenbo decided to donate 30 million yuan to set up a foundation with the purpose of promoting Go. After consulting with Lin Haifeng, a national player, he received his full support and promised to serve as the first chairman. The "Haifeng Cultural and Educational Foundation" was established and started the career of promoting Weiqi. Since organizing professional and amateur competitions requires a lot of money and manpower, the Haifeng Foundation has been donated by Chairman Lin Wenbo for many years, and has grown rapidly. However, professional competitions are still insufficient. Therefore, there is a need for organizational adjustments. Director Lin The president decided to merge the "Haifeng Cultural and Educational Foundation" into the "Pearson Cultural and Educational Foundation" in order to play the role of one plus one greater than two.

The Pearson Cultural and Educational Foundation is another consortium legal person donated by Chairman Lin Wenbo and other families, and has been engaged in special education work. Since the funds are more abundant than the Haifeng Cultural and Educational Foundation, after the "Haifeng Cultural and Educational Foundation" was merged into the "Pearson Cultural and Educational Foundation", the Haifeng Cultural and Educational Foundation retired successfully, and the stick to promote chess was handed over to the "Pearson Cultural and Educational Foundation". meeting". In order to meet the needs of Go work, the staff of the original Haifeng Cultural and Educational Foundation have also been adjusted and reorganized as "Haifeng Chess Institute" to continue to promote Go on the front line.
